## Why

The `project.md` outlines a modern, smart digital clinical platform (Medical Booking). To implement this, we need a detailed analysis of the necessary features and screens, organizing them logically for both end-users (individuals/businesses) and internal staff (admins/doctors). This change proposes a structured breakdown of all required screens and functionalities.

## What Changes

- Detailed screen-by-screen breakdown of the Front-end (Homepage, Services, Booking, Smart Features, News).
- Detailed screen-by-screen breakdown of the Back-end/Admin Panel (Schedule management, CRM, CMS, Reports).
- Identification of core capabilities that drive the functionality behind these screens.
- Separation of concerns between public informative pages, interactive patient portals, and administrative management.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `client-portal`: Covers public pages like Home, Services, News, and patient-specific views like Electronic Health Records (EHR) and Online Pharmacy.
- `booking-engine`: Core system to handle appointment scheduling for clinics and home visits, including real-time slot selection.
- `telemedicine`: Remote patient consultation via chat or video call.
- `admin-dashboard`: System for staff to manage appointments, patient records (CRM), content (CMS), and system reports.
